Doing a Startup

Here is a way of doing a startup (a temporary organization in search of a repeatable and scalable business model)

Ingredients

  • Market
  • Product
  • Team

Steps

  1. Find a market that you are passionate about
  2. Find a problem within that market
  3. Talk with customers
  4. Build something customers want
  5. Build a team that will grow from zero (startup) to one (business)
