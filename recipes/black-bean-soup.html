Black Bean Soup

Here is how you can cook a delicious black bean soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of black beans
  • 6 cups of water
    • 1 teaspoon of Basil
  • 1 teaspoon of Oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper
  • 3/4 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Pour the water and beans in a big enough pan
  2. Add all other ingredients
  3. Cook the beans for about two hours at a very low (lowest) temperature
  4. Serve the soup by adding cheese (Queso Fresco), cream (Crema Mexicana), chopped onion, and rice
